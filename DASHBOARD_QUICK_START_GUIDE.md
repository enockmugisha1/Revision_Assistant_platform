## Troubleshooting

### "Dashboard shows no data"
- You haven't taken any quizzes yet
- Take your first quiz to populate stats
- Set some study goals

### "AI features not showing"
- AI may not be connected
- Contact admin to enable AI features
- Platform still fully functional without AI

### "Stats not updating"
- Refresh the page
- Complete more activities
- Check your internet connection
